--- title: Free web-based interface for TFS available now! date: '2007-03-26T20:57:00.000+02:00' tags: modified_time: '2007-03-26T20:58:14.290+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-15137028.post-1399024383266651125 blogger_orig_url: http://www.continuousimprover.com/2007/03/free-web-based-interface-for-tfs.html --- This is certainly incredible. One day after I blogged about my complaints on Team Foundation Server (especially its lack of a web-based interface), Microsoft announced that they've acquired DevBiz Business Solutions, the creators of TeamPlain, the best commercial TFS-based product available. And what's best, you can download it for free starting right now! I'm sure going to try it first thing in the morning.

Oh yes, and not to forget, Microsoft released a new roadmap for Visual Studio Team System, including the post-"Orcas" product, codenamed Rosario.
