It's a pattern language for common solutions for integrating cross-platform application and systems. BizTalk is one of those products dedicating their lives to enterprise integration. This great article written by people from ThoughtWorks provides a nice real-life example of usage of those patterns and possible implementation using BizTalk 2004.
