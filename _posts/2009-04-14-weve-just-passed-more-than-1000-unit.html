--- title: We've just passed more than 1000 unit tests date: '2009-04-14T12:35:00.003+02:00' tags: modified_time: '2009-04-14T12:43:13.538+02:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_sv2gnsft17w/SeRofdX3DQI/AAAAAAAAC_4/0zxY5sWpfbE/s72-c/1003unittests.png blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-15137028.post-8305357685443499928 blogger_orig_url: http://www.continuousimprover.com/2009/04/weve-just-passed-more-than-1000-unit.html --- In November 2008, me and some fellow colleagues have started working on a new project where we use Test Driven Development to gain a highly maintainable and highly testable system. Today, we have reached our 1000st unit test, or better, 'specification'. The term Test Driven is a bit misleading since TDD is really a design process, which has nice little granular unit tests as a side-effect. Anyway, we tried to reach this point before Eastern, but obviously we were professional enough not to add some dummy tests :-)
