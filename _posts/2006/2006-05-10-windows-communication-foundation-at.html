It provides a tip of the iceberg on what you can do with WCF. For those who don’t have a clue of what I’m talking about, WCF is Microsoft’s attempt to unite all their communication protocols into one uniform communication stack. On turn, this stack is part of WinFX, the next-generation managed APIs by Microsoft.
