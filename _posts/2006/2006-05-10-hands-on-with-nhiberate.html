--- title: Hands-on with NHiberate date: '2006-05-10T09:07:00.000+02:00' tags: modified_time: '2006-05-10T09:08:46.816+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-15137028.post-114724492680363635 blogger_orig_url: http://www.continuousimprover.com/2006/05/hands-on-with-nhiberate.html --- You may have heard of NHiberate, the .NET variant of the famous Hibernate product, and you may understand the concept of an Object-Relational Mapper. But you may not have a clear picture of what it really can and does. Justin Gehtland published an article on TheServerSide.net that gives a quick introduction on how to configure NHibernate to map a domain model to a database model. The examples include one-to-many and many-to-many relations and inheritance.
